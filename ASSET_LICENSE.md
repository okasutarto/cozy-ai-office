# Asset License Information

The public fallback office PNGs come from 2dPig's Pixel Office Asset Pack,
and the animated character layers come from Ordinary Bumblebee's 32x32
Customizable Character Pack.
Both are released under the Creative Commons CC0 1.0 Public Domain
Dedication. The vendored licenses and source notes are kept in
`art/vendor/pixel-office/` and `art/vendor/ordinary-bumblebee/`.

Source: <https://2dpig.itch.io/pixel-office>

Character source: <https://ordinary-bumblebee.itch.io/customizable-character-pack>

Pixel Life office sources and generated office skins are local-only, ignored by
Git, and are not part of the distributed asset set.

License: <https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/>

Attribution is not required under CC0, but this repository retains the author
credit and records the imported files, transformations, and generated hashes
in `public/assets/licenses.json`.

## Asset Manifest & Compliance

All generated assets must be listed in `public/assets/asset-manifest.json` and
their source/license records must remain synchronized in `public/assets/licenses.json`.
Proprietary assets or resources copied from third-party games are prohibited.
